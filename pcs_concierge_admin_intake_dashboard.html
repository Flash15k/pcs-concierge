Automation logic

After submission, this dashboard should automatically receive the intake data from the client portal. Once a VA reviews it, the system should support three actions:

1. Trigger orientation-call scheduling email
2. Create the client record in your master dashboard / CRM
3. Push the client into needs mapping and action-plan workflow

Build notes for your developer

Best connected stack: client intake portal to Airtable or CRM to admin dashboard.

This HTML is designed as the operations-side companion to the branded intake portal. It should feel visually connected to the client side while giving your VA practical control over intake triage, assignment, and transition into service delivery.
